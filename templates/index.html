About Brain Tumor Detection

Brain tumors are abnormal growths of cells in the brain that can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). Early detection is crucial for effective treatment and improved outcomes.

This web application uses Artificial Intelligence to help doctors and patients detect the presence of a brain tumor from MRI images.

  • Upload a brain MRI scan along with patient details.
  • Our AI model will analyze the image and predict the likelihood of a tumor.
  • All predictions are saved for future reference and can be reviewed in the history section.
